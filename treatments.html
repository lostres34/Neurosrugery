Treatments & Procedures

The Neurosurgery Department offers a comprehensive range of treatments for brain, spine, and peripheral nerve disorders using the latest technology and surgical techniques.

Brain Surgeries

  • Brain Tumor Resection (Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors)
  • Aneurysm Clipping and Coiling
  • AVM Resection (Arteriovenous Malformations)
  • Hydrocephalus Treatment (Ventriculoperitoneal Shunt)
  • Craniotomy for Traumatic Brain Injury
  • Functional Neurosurgery (e.g. Epilepsy surgery)

Spine Surgeries

  • Discectomy and Microdiscectomy
  • Laminectomy and Spinal Decompression
  • Spinal Fusion Surgery
  • Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty
  • Spinal Tumor Resection
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS)

Other Specialized Services

  • Peripheral Nerve Surgery (e.g. Carpal Tunnel Release)
  • Endoscopic Neurosurgery
  • Neurotrauma and Emergency Neurosurgical Care
  • Intraoperative Neuromonitoring
  • Multidisciplinary Neuro-oncology Care
