About Us

The Neurosurgery Department at Mahdi Clinic is committed to providing exceptional care in neurosurgery with a strong foundation in research, innovation, and education.

Our Mission

To deliver state-of-the-art neurosurgical services with compassion and integrity, while advancing knowledge through research and training.

Vision

To be recognized as a national leader in neurosurgical excellence and a hub for medical education and discovery.

Core Values

  • Patient-centered care
  • Clinical excellence
  • Ethics and empathy
  • Innovation in practice
  • Collaborative teamwork

History

Established over two decades ago, our department has grown into one of the most respected neurosurgery centers in Iran, known for handling complex cases with modern approaches.

Achievements

We have performed over 10,000 successful procedures and published 200+ scientific papers, contributing significantly to neurosurgical knowledge in the region.
